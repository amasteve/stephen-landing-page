Stephen Egar Ayim is committed to advancing inclusive growth and civic engagement in Cross River State. As Ward Rural Development Officer for Ekajuk Ward 1, he plays an active role in community sensitization, policy communication, and grassroots development initiatives that improve the welfare of local residents.

As Founder of Amasteve Integrated Services and Executive Publisher of Ogoja Breaking News, he drives digital innovation, community storytelling, youth empowerment, and socio-economic development across the region.

With expertise spanning digital media, public communication, branding, and community engagement, Stephen is passionate about bridging the gap between leadership and the people — through effective information dissemination and practical, development-focused advocacy.

He is widely recognized for his commitment to grassroots inclusion, youth development, and building platforms that promote transparency, empowerment, and sustainable progress.
